Our Investment Sectors

Bestchem operates in 12 key sectors across Zimbabwe and the African region. We focus on growth, sustainability, and value delivery in each area.

Mining Development

We invest in sustainable and responsible mining solutions, covering exploration, extraction, and value addition across Zimbabwe’s rich mineral zones.

Civil & Construction

Our construction arm builds modern infrastructure from residential housing to highways and commercial projects, fostering national development.

Energy

We power the future with renewable and thermal energy investments that support off-grid communities and national electricity supply goals.

General Trading

Our trading division supplies essential goods, industrial products, and materials across Southern Africa through a reliable distribution network.

Real Estate Development

From affordable housing to luxurious estates and industrial complexes, we develop real estate solutions that meet modern demands.

Detergents Manufacturing

We produce high-quality cleaning products that serve homes, hospitals, institutions, and industries under strict manufacturing standards.

Agro-Processing & Agribusiness

Bestchem supports food security through agricultural production, processing, packaging, and value chain development across key commodities.

Logistics & Transportation

We provide professional logistics services for domestic and cross-border deliveries, from freight and bulk goods to courier and express transport.

Industrial Manufacturing

We support industrial growth by manufacturing equipment, machinery parts, and essential industrial goods for Zimbabwe and the SADC region.

Healthcare Solutions

Bestchem improves access to health services through investments in medical supplies, diagnostic tools, hospital infrastructure, and pharmaceuticals.

Furniture Manufacturing

We manufacture durable and modern furniture for offices, schools, and homes — blending quality craftsmanship with affordability.

Technology & ICT

We power digital transformation with smart tech, hardware, software, and connectivity services for institutions and enterprises.
